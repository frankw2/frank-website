DBWipes: better and deeper tools for interactive analytics

As datasets and analyses become larger more complex, even making sense of query results becomes very difficult.

DBWipes is a collection of projects to make data analysis interactive and powerful.

Scorpion

Consider a simple analytic query that computes a company's total expenses by month, and shows that last month's expenses was unexpectedly high. The analyst will naturally want to understand why -- perhaps the company has put more resources into a new customer demographic, or a department is overspending.

Currently, the analyst must manually split the input data along different dimensions (e.g., dept, customer age), and hope that re-running the query will cause changes. If there is more than one outlier, or many dimensions in the dataset, this ad-hoc process quickly becomes untenable.

Scorpion is an interactive system to answer these "why" questions. The analysts simply selects outlier and normal results and the system constructs predicates that potentially explain the outliers. To find such predicates, we develop a notion of influence of a predicate on the outputs, and design efficient algorithms to find the most influential predicates
